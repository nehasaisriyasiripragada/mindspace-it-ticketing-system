Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can I get an appointment?

Most clients can schedule an initial consultation within 48 hours. Urgent cases are prioritized and often seen the same day.

Do you accept insurance?

We work with most major insurance providers including BlueCross, Aetna, and Cigna. Our team can verify your benefits before your first session.

What if I need help outside business hours?

Our crisis line (555-987-6543) is available 24/7 for urgent mental health needs. For non-urgent matters, you can email us and we'll respond within 12 hours.

Can I switch therapists if it's not a good fit?

Absolutely. We encourage you to find the therapist who best meets your needs. Our care coordinators can help match you with someone else at no additional cost.
