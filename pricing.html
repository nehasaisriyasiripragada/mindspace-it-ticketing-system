Pricing FAQs

Do you offer sliding scale fees?

Yes, we offer income-based sliding scale fees starting at $60/session for those who qualify. Contact our support team with proof of income to apply.

What's your cancellation policy?

We require 24 hours notice for cancellations. Late cancellations are charged 50% of the session fee, except in emergencies.

Are there any additional fees?

Initial intake sessions are $20 more due to additional paperwork. All other fees are included in the session price unless otherwise discussed.

Do you offer package discounts?

Yes! Purchasing 4 sessions upfront gives you 5% off, and 8 sessions gives you 10% off. Packages must be used within 6 months.
